About Me

I am Guilherme dos Santos Mendonça Enéas, a Software Engineer focused on backend development in Java and distributed systems architecture. I have over 2 years of hands-on experience building RESTful APIs, microservices, and scalable solutions, applying Clean Code, SOLID, DDD, and TDD in real-world projects.

My journey includes freelance work on Workana and the development of applications across different sectors (education, sports, aesthetics), with emphasis on internal communication systems with email sending, task scheduling, and automated notifications, as well as well-documented APIs, tested and integrated into CI/CD pipelines with Docker.

I have solid experience with Java, Spring Boot, Kafka, RabbitMQ, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Docker, GitHub Actions, and automated testing (JUnit5/Mockito). My workflow is always driven by engineering best practices, modern architectural patterns, and a focus on delivering value.

I am currently in the final year of my degree in Systems Analysis and Development at UniCesumar, and I am preparing for a postgraduate program in Software Architecture and possibly a master's degree in Computer Science.

At the same time, I am expanding my stack by studying Kotlin and Go, and deepening my knowledge in Cloud Computing (AWS/Azure/Google Cloud), DevOps, and AI.

My goal is to take on strategic roles such as Java Backend Developer or Fullstack Developer, contributing to high-impact projects involving architecture, cloud, and distributed systems always in agile, collaborative environments committed to technical excellence.
